Lynne and Michael's Treasure Hunt

Overview

This is a game in which your goal is to find certain objects around our home.

You will figure out which objects to find (and sometimes also where they might be located) by solving rhyming clues.

If you get stuck then you can ask for a hint.

If you still can't figure it out with the hint then you can ask for what item you are looking for to be revealed ("scavenger hunt mode").

Independent of hints, you can also skip a clue to come back to it later. The details of skipping are given below.

You will earn points depending on how quickly you find the answer and how much help you ask for. The details of scoring are presented below.

Skipping

The rules for skipping are as follows:

  • Each clue can be skipped one time
  • You must spend at least ${{MIN_SKIP_DURATION}} working on a clue before skipping
  • You will not return to a skipped clue until you have seen all other clues
  • If you skip a clue a second time then you will not see it again and earn 0 points for that clue

Scoring

Scoring is determined as follows:

  • 300 points for solving a clue without any hint
  • 200 points for solving a clue with using the hint
  • 100 points for solving a clue in scavenger hunt mode

Bonus points are given based on time. Up to 100 bonus points are available per clue, subject to an exponential decay with a half life of 10 minutes.

For example: Suppose a team is able to solve a clue in 7 minutes using a hint, then they receive 262 points (200 from solving with a hint pluse 62 for solving in 7 minutes).
